## When to Add Here

Add an ADR when:
- Making a significant architectural or design decision
- Choosing between multiple technical approaches
- Establishing a new pattern or convention
- Deprecating or changing an existing decision

## Numbering

ADRs are numbered sequentially: ADR-0001, ADR-0002, etc.
